今天吃啥 · Spin the wheel, dinner decided

Can't decide what to eat? Yumspin puts real restaurants around you — or your own options — on a wheel of fortune. One spin, and the choice is made.

🎡 Two wheels

Spin real nearby eateries found via Apple Maps, or build a custom wheel of your own choices — works with no location at all.

📍 Pick any spot

Use your location, drop a pin anywhere on the map, or search an address — then spin that neighbourhood.

🍜 Filter by craving

Chinese, Japanese & Korean, Southeast Asian, Western, fast food, cafés & tea, desserts — narrow the wheel to what you feel like.

🚫 Not interested

Block places you don't fancy for good, and restore them any time from the blocklist.
